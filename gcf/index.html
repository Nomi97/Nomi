    Software

    We focus primarily on your business goals and we try to find the most expedient way to meet your requirements, in whatever form they come. We approach you, as a customer, as an opportunity to bring your ideas and solutions forth and acting more like a partner than the typical consulting company. Meaning that we hope to use your existing resources and develop stable software platforms that you can expand upon as the need arises.Long time solutions that enables us to grow our own company and expertise alongside yours. In order to achive the best expected results and user-friendly interactions. At the same time making sure that the right confidentiality is applied according to your needs..

    Approach

    When we create your software solution we aim to make it so that you can use it in the future without our help. That means that each application or server solution is made with in-depth architectural design, planning, code testing and user testing so that the software becomes intuitive to use and reduces the need for support lines for everyday use.

    Development needs

    While we can build whatever your company needs, our expertise lies in building business applications for the Windows platform using the .NET framework and SQL. This doesn’t mean that we’re locked to these technologies and as the need arises we can expand into mobile and web applications as well. If you’re especially interested we can even provide you with cross-platform solutions for Windows 10 that covers Desktops, Tablets, Windows phones, the Xbox One and the coming HoloLens

    Methodology

    We believe that software development should be a learning experience and that every line of code should be an exemplar for later developers to follow and improve upon. This reflects upon your company as we believe in transparency when it comes to develop software that suits your need. You, as a client, should have the option to view the progress of the project, getting bug lists and deciding at early stages how the project should move forward, as well as on-going future developments as the software matures. We don’t just provide you with milestone builds, but offer an often-needed interactivity between your company and our software developers to minimize risk of erroneous development of features and methods, as well as bringing new ideas, user interactivity and features into the light.
